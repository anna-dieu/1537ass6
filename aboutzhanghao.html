About Zhang Hao 章昊

Violinist + Music Teacher + Member of ZB1

Published on January 15, 2024 by Anna

Zhang Hao, also known as 章昊 (Zhāng Hào) in Chinese, was born on July 25, 2000. He is a Chinese singer currently based in South Korea and is signed under Yuehua Entertainment. Zhang Hao rose to prominence by clinching the top spot in the reality competition series Boys Planet. This victory propelled him to debut as a member of the resulting boy group, ZB1 known as Zerobaseone, in July 2023. Notably, Zhang Hao made history as the first foreigner to achieve first place in a South Korean idol survival show of this nature. Prior to his debut and appearance on the reality competition, he worked as a violinist and certified music teacher.


Facts

  • Date of Birth: July 25, 2000
  • Place of Birth: Fujian, China (Nanping)
  • Education: Fujian Normal University
  • Major: Music
  • Hobby: Food Tour / Restaurant Tour, swimming, walking, travelling
  • Languages: Chinese, Korean, English
